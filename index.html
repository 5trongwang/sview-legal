Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my Pro subscription?

Open SView → tap the settings icon → tap "Restore Purchases". Your subscription will be restored automatically if you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

Does SView upload my photos?

No. All photo processing is done entirely on your device. We never upload, store, or access your photos on any server.

Which photo formats are supported?

SView supports all standard iOS photo formats including JPEG, PNG, HEIC, and wide-format panoramic photos.

I was charged but Pro features aren't unlocked. What should I do?

Please use "Restore Purchases" first. If the issue persists, contact us at support@sviewapp.com with your Apple transaction ID and we'll resolve it promptly.
